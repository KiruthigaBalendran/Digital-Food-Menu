How to Place an Order

Steps

1. Click the Menu tab.

2. Go through the list of food item

3. Click 'Read More' button.

4. Customize according to your preference.

5. Click 'Add to Cart'.

6. For multiple orders click 'Continue Shopping'.

7. Repeat steps 2-5.

8. Click Proceed to Checkout.

9. Select Payment method.

10.Click 'Proceed'.
